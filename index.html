Why stringing matters

Dead strings lose you matches.

A poly string can lose up to 10% of its tension in the first 24 hours — and most club players are rallying on strings that are weeks past their prime. The result? Flyers on big points, wrist pain, and a racket that doesn't feel like yours anymore.

I string with a constant-pull machine, clamp with care, and will talk you through which string actually matches your game — not just what's on sale.
