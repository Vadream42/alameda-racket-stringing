Questions

What to expect.

Where do you coach?

Anywhere with a public court in Alameda — and I'll travel to the greater Bay Area for regular lessons. Tell me your preferred court when we book.

What about court fees?

Alameda's public courts are free. If you want a reserved private court, that's on you to book and pay for.

How many players can do a group?

Up to four on one court works well. Same $80/hour total — split it however you want among the players.

Cancellations?

Just let me know ahead of time. Weather cancels — we'll reschedule, no charge.

Want to book a lesson?

Call or text to set up your first session. First lesson, I'll take 10 minutes up front to figure out what you want to get better at — and the rest is all court time.
